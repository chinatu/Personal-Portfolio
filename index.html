What I do

As a QA Engineer, I ensure that our software products meet the highest quality standards possible. I use my analytical and detail-oriented approach to develop and execute comprehensive test plans, utilizing a range of technologies such as Javascript, Java, Cypress, NoSQL (MongoDB), SQL, Appium, Postman, Selenium (Java) and more.

My work involves conducting functional, integration, cross-platform, and exploratory testing of new product features. I also design and execute performance testing strategies, and work with other QA engineers to develop framework solutions for API automation. Additionally, I excel at detecting and reporting bugs, writing test cases, and implementing test automation monitoring initiatives in both API and UI automation scenarios.

With my Certified Tester Foundation Level (CTFL) certification from ISTQB, and A Silicon Valley Immersion Program in San Mateo, California, and a Degree in Computer Science, I bring a strong foundation in software testing methodology and industry best practices to my work. I am confident in my ability to lead requirements gathering for new product features, as well as support ongoing work on manual documentation of functional quality assurance tests.
